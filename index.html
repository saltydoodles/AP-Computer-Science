Defence

I know it may not be the first thing that comes to mind when it comes to fighting games, but it's the most important thing you'll learn.

Blocking

There are two kinds of blocking in most fighting games. First off is stand blocking, which is performed by holding back (or 4 in keypad notations).

The second kind is low block, done by holding down and back (or 1 in keypad notations).
